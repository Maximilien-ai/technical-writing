## Content Pipeline

```
Outline → Editor Review → Draft → Fact Check → Revision → Publish
  (Drafts)   (Drafts)    (Drafts)  (Review)   (Drafts)  (Publishing)
```

## Workflow

1. **Writer** submits outline to Drafts group
2. **Editor** reviews and approves outline (or requests changes)
3. **Writer** produces first draft, posts to Drafts
4. **Editor** does editorial pass, then moves to Review group
5. **Reviewer** fact-checks, tests code, verifies accuracy
6. **Writer** addresses review feedback
7. **Editor** final approval
8. **Publisher** formats, adds metadata, publishes
9. **Publisher** confirms publication in Status group

## Deadlines

- Outlines due: 2 days after assignment
- First draft due: 5 days after outline approval
- Review turnaround: 2 days
- Revision turnaround: 2 days
- Publication: 1 day after final approval
