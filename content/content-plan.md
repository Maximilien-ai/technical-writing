# Content Plan — Q2 2026

**Owner:** Editor | **Created:** 2026-03-27 | **Updated:** 2026-03-27

## Mission

Produce high-quality technical content for developers on **AI assistants**, **OpenClaw**, and **AI agents**. Every piece should be accurate, actionable, and accessible at a college reading level.

## Target Audience

Developers — junior to senior. Comfortable with code, curious about AI tooling and agent architectures. May or may not have used AI assistants or built agents before.

## Topic Areas

### 1. AI Assistants
How developers can effectively use AI coding assistants (Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code, etc.) in their daily workflow.

### 2. OpenClaw
What OpenClaw is, how to set it up, and how to build with it. Tutorials, architecture, and practical guides.

### 3. AI Agents
The broader AI agent landscape — what agents are, how they work, design patterns, and where the field is heading.
